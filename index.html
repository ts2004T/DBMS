About

In today's academic setting, prioritizing student mental health is crucial. To address this, we propose developing a Student Wellness and Counseling System (SWCS). SWCS will serve as a centralized platform, facilitating easy access to support services and resources. Through intuitive dashboards, SWCS empowers students to manage their well-being effectively. Its significance lies in fostering a culture of wellness and contributing to students' holistic development.
